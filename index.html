who am I?

Nobody can truly answer that question, but my attempt would be that I'm currently a Junior Web Developer at Mississippi Coding Academies. Depending on who you ask, I'm an introvert or an extrovert, but I really prefer to keep to myself. I read a lot of manga, I enjoy narrative analysis and I write my own webnovel, with art by my girlfriend. Since I was a kid, I've been messing with computer code. If you know about M.U.G.E.N, that was where my interest was born. I liked video games a lot and the idea of being able to make my own with people from around the world got me into problem solving through changing lines of code. It was annoying, but it was always rewarding when it worked out. I had a knack for finding solutions.
